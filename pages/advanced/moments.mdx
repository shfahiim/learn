# Moments and MGFs

## Moments
The $n$-th moment of a random variable $X$ is defined as $E[X^n]$. 
- The 1st moment is the Mean.
- The 2nd central moment is the Variance.

## Moment Generating Functions (MGF)
The MGF is a magical function $M_X(t) = E[e^{tX}]$. If you can find the MGF, you can find any moment of the distribution by taking its derivative and evaluating it at $t=0$.
