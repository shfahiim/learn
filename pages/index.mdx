# Master Guide to Probability Distributions

Welcome to the comprehensive, step-by-step guide to mastering probability distributions!

This guide is structured from beginner concepts to advanced applications, with a strong focus on intuitive understanding and visualization. 

Use the sidebar to navigate through the different phases of the learning journey:

1. **Foundations**: The core building blocks of probability and statistics.
2. **Discrete Distributions**: Focusing on counting events (e.g., coin flips, website clicks).
3. **Continuous Distributions**: Focusing on measuring things (e.g., heights, time).
4. **Advanced Distributions**: Used in specialized statistical testing and Bayesian inference.
5. **"God Tier" Concepts**: The theorems that connect everything together (LLN, CLT).
